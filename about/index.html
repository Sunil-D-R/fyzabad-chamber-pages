Empowering Local Businesses, Driving Community Growth

We are a local organization dedicated to supporting and promoting businesses in Fyzabad and beyond. Our membership includes a diverse range of businesses, from small start-ups to established companies across a variety of industries. Our goal is to provide our members with the linkages to resources, networking opportunities, and advocacy they need to succeed and thrive.

At The Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce, we believe in the power of collaboration with businesses, communities and other stakeholders. We are committed to fostering an environment where members can connect with other local businesses, share ideas and best practices, and work together to address common challenges and opportunities.

As a member-driven organization, we are always looking for ways to better serve our members and promote economic growth and development in Trinidad and Tobago. Our activities include business workshops, providing educational resources and training, advocating for our members and hosting networking events.

Our Services

The Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce offers a range of valuable services to its members, including:

  • Networking opportunities
  • Advocacy and representation
  • Business resources and support
  • Group Health Plan
  • Educational workshops and training
  • Business development resources

Our Core Values

The Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce is guided by a set of core values that reflect our commitment to supporting and empowering local businesses.

Community Connection and Networking

We are deeply connected to the Fyzabad community, the surrounding areas and by extension Trinidad and Tobago. Our network of contacts and partnerships can help you tap into new opportunities and markets.

Education and Training

Our educational workshops and training programs are designed to improve your business skills. We provide opportunities to learn from professionals, industry experts and successful business leaders.

Advocacy and Support

We advocate for the needs and concerns of the local business communities. We work to create a business-friendly environment that supports growth and prosperity.

Our Mission

Our mission at The Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce is to promote economic growth and development by providing linkages to access resources, networking opportunities, training education and advocacy for our members. We believe in fostering a strong sense of collaboration with businesses, communities and other stakeholders to address common challenges and opportunities.

Our Vision

Our vision at The Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce is to connect businesses to inspire communities by creating a vibrant, thriving business community that drives economic growth and development in Fyzabad and by extension Trinidad and Tobago.
