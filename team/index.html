Ms. Angenie Jairam

President

About Ms. Jairam

Angenie Jairam, often known as Ms Jairam, is deeply committed to making a significant impact in the healthcare sector. As a Medical Gas Specialist and Certified Medical Gas Inspector with expertise in Disease and Infection Control, she is driven by the mission to ensure safety and excellence for patients. She specializes in designing, supplying, and installing medical gas equipment that adheres to the rigorous standards and regulations in Trinidad and Tobago and internationally.

Professional Experience

With over 30 years of diverse experience, Ms Jairam’s career journey spans multiple sectors:

  • Banking
  • Family business
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Medical equipment

Entrepreneurial Success

Fifteen years ago, she founded two successful companies:

  • DNA & Associates Ltd.
  • Ms Jairam Enterprises

Today, these companies proudly serve health institutions in Trinidad and Tobago and abroad, focusing on quality and innovation in the healthcare sector.

Expertise

  • Medical Gas Specialist
  • Certified Medical Gas Inspector
  • Disease and Infection Control Expert
  • Healthcare Equipment Design and Installation
  • Medical Standards Compliance

As President of the Fyzabad Chamber of Commerce, Ms. Jairam brings her extensive business experience and leadership skills to support and promote local business development.
